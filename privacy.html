Trading Ledger — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Trading Ledger ("the app") is a personal trading journal that runs entirely in your web browser. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. The app has no backend server and its operators do not collect, receive, store, or have access to your data.

What data the app handles

  • Your trading entries (trades, options, spreads, futures, prices, notes, account labels) that you type into the app.
  • App preferences such as your selected theme.

Where your data is stored

  • In your browser by default, using local storage on your own device. It never leaves your device unless you turn on Google Drive sync.
  • In your own Google Drive, only if you choose to connect it (optional). When connected, the app saves a single file, trading-ledger.json, to your Drive so your journal can follow you across your devices.

Google user data

If you connect Google Drive, the app requests the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file permission. This grants the app access only to the files it creates or that you open with it — it cannot see or access any other files in your Google Drive.

  • How it's used: solely to create, read, and update your own trading-ledger.json file so your ledger syncs between your devices.
  • Where it goes: directly between your browser and your own Google Drive. It is not sent to, processed by, or stored on any server operated by the app's developer.
  • Sharing: your data is never sold or shared with anyone. No third parties receive it.
  • Limited Use: the app's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements.

Analytics & tracking

None. The app contains no analytics, advertising, trackers, or cookies for tracking. (It may load Google's sign-in library and Google Fonts from Google's servers when you open it.)

Your control

  • Export / delete locally: use the app's Data tab to export a backup or clear all data at any time.
  • Disconnect Drive: use "Disconnect" in the app, and/or revoke the app's access at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Delete the Drive file: delete trading-ledger.json from your own Google Drive whenever you wish.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue at github.com/THESHULK/Trading-Ledger/issues.

Trading Ledger is open-source software you run yourself. Because there is no central server, you remain in control of your own data at all times.
